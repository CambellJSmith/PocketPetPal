how_it_works

Care is now hands-on inside the main pet screen. Drag food onto the creature’s mouth, rub dirt away directly across the creature with the sponge, and apply medicine to the creature without opening a separate care window. If hunger, boredom, exhaustion, dirt, or sickness are neglected for too long, health can reach zero and the creature dies. Everything still saves locally in the player’s browser using localStorage.
