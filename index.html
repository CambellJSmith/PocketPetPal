Care is now hands-on. Drag food to the creature’s mouth, rub dirt away with the sponge, and apply medicine directly to the safe spot. If hunger, boredom, exhaustion, dirt, or sickness are neglected for too long, health can reach zero and the creature dies. Everything still saves locally in the player’s browser using localStorage.
